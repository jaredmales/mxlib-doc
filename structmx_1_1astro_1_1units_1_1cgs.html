struct mx::astro::units::cgs< _realT >

Centimeter-Gram-Second (cgs) units-type.

The units are:

  • Length: centimeter (cm)
  • Time: second (s)
  • Mass: gram (g)
  • Energy: erg (erg)
  • Temperature: Kelvin (K)
