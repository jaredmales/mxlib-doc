struct mx::AO::analysis::pywfsModAsymptotic< realT, iosT >

The asymptotic modulated pyramid wavefront sensor sensitivity function.

Provides the \( \beta_p \) parameter of Guyon, 2005 [8] for the modulated PyWFS in the asymptotic limit.

Template Parameters
realTis the floating point type used for calculations
